Food Vlogs

Tupig

Tupig, also known as intemtem or kangkanen, is a Filipino rice cake originating from northwestern Luzon, particularly the region of Pangasinan It is made from ground slightly-fermented soaked glutinous rice (galapong) mixed with coconut milk, muscovado sugar, and young coconut (buko) strips.

Kaleskes

Kaleskes is a dish from Pangasinan prepared with carabao or cows' intestine, pancreas, tripe and other internal organs cooked in a soup form seasoned with vinegar and other spices

Pigar-Pigar

Pigar-pigar, meaning to mix is a popular delicacy in Pangasinan. It is traditionally made of thin slices of marinated carabeef & sauteed with spices, cauliflower & cabbage.

Seafood feast

Famous actress Angeline Quinto went to Dagupan seafood market and did seafod mukbang!

Alaminos Longanisa

Filipino pork sausage originating from Alaminos, Pangasinan. It is a type of de recado longganisa. The segments of the sausage are uniquely divided by small pieces of coconut leaf midribs (sometimes mistaken for toothpicks).
